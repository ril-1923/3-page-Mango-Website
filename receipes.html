Mango Lassi

A refreshing Indian yogurt drink blended with ripe mangoes, a touch of cardamom, and a hint of sweetness. Perfect for hot summer days or as a complement to spicy meals.

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar or honey
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Ice cubes
Easy
🥤

Mango Sticky Rice

A beloved Thai dessert featuring sweet glutinous rice cooked in coconut milk, served with perfectly ripe mango slices and a drizzle of coconut cream.

  • 1 cup glutinous (sticky) rice
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes, sliced
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch (for thickening)
Medium
🍚

Fresh Mango Salsa

A vibrant, zesty salsa that pairs beautifully with grilled fish, tacos, or as a standalone dip. The sweetness of mango balances perfectly with lime and chili.

  • 2 ripe mangoes, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, minced
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt to taste
Easy
🌶️

Mango Cheesecake

A creamy, no-bake cheesecake with a luscious mango puree topping. This dessert is elegant enough for special occasions yet simple enough for a weekend treat.

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped
  • 2 cups mango puree
  • 1 tbsp gelatin
Hard
