Mango Varieties

Explore the incredible diversity of mangoes from around the world.

Popular Varieties

Alphonso (Hapus)

Known as the "King of Mangoes," the Alphonso is prized for its rich, creamy texture and intense sweetness. It has a vibrant saffron-colored flesh and a distinctive aroma that makes it one of the most sought-after varieties in the world.

India Maharashtra Premium
🥭

Ataulfo (Honey Mango)

Small, golden, and buttery smooth, the Ataulfo mango has a thin seed and velvety flesh. It is incredibly sweet with honey-like notes and virtually no fibers, making it perfect for smoothies and fresh eating.

Mexico Yellow Sweet
🍯

Haden

One of the most widely cultivated varieties, the Haden mango has a bright red and green skin with a sweet, rich flavor. It is a classic choice for mango lovers and is commonly found in supermarkets worldwide.

Florida USA Classic
🌴

Kesar

Named for its saffron-like color ("Kesar" means saffron), this variety from Gujarat, India, is known for its sharp sweetness and intense pulp. It is widely used in making mango pulp and desserts.

India Gujarat Pulp Favorite
