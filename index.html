Did You Know?

Over 1,000 Varieties

There are more than 1,000 different varieties of mangoes grown worldwide, each with its own unique flavor, texture, and color. From the creamy Alphonso to the fibrous Haden, every mango tells a different story.

Mangoes are native to South Asia, particularly India and Myanmar, and have been cultivated for over 4,000 years. Today, they are grown in tropical and subtropical regions across the globe.

National Fruit of India

The mango holds the prestigious title of being the national fruit of India, Pakistan, and the Philippines. It is deeply woven into the cultural fabric of these nations, featuring in festivals, art, and cuisine.

In India alone, over 20 million metric tons of mangoes are produced annually, making it the world's largest producer.
